# Privacy Policy for Ruta Taxi

**Last updated: May 7, 2026**

## Introduction

Ruta Taxi ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Shift Records**: Date, start time, end time, shift hours, total money, expenses
- **Expense Records**: Expense name, amount, date, notes
- **App Settings**: Working days per week, monthly target, gas percentage, tax percentage, language preference

### Data Automatically Collected
- **Device Information**: Device model, Android version
- **Advertising ID**: For Yandex Ads to show relevant advertisements
- **Crash Reports**: To help us fix bugs and improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

- To save and display your shift and expense records
- To calculate your earnings, net profit, and statistics
- To backup and restore your data (when you use these features)
- To export your data to CSV format
- To show advertisements (Yandex Ads)
- To improve app performance and fix bugs

## Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your personal data or shift records to any external servers.

- **Database**: Stored locally on your device
- **Backup files**: Stored in your device's Downloads folder (when you choose to backup)
- **Export files**: Stored in your device's Downloads folder (when you export CSV)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties. The only third-party service we use is:

- **Yandex Ads**: Shows advertisements in the app. Yandex may collect advertising ID and device information. See [Yandex Privacy Policy](https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Delete all your data** using the "Delete All Data" button in Settings
- **Export your data** using the "Export Data" feature
- **Backup and restore** your data using the Backup/Restore feature
- **Uninstall the app** to remove all local data

## Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Why we need it |
|------------|----------------|
| WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE | To save backup files and export CSV files |
| READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE | To restore backup files |
| INTERNET | To load advertisements and show ads |
| ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE | To check internet connection for ads |

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings tab
3. Click "DELETE ALL DATA"
4. Confirm deletion

All your shifts, expenses, and settings will be permanently deleted from your device.
